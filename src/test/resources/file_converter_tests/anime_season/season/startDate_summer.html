Dr. Stone was a thrilling ride. This anime was among the best for its airing season. The premise is a roundabout isekai (HEAR ME OUT!!!). The main antagonist is a wiz-kid scientist. The real world gets turned to stone and the MC breaks free from petrification thousands of years later. At which point he becomes one of the smartest people alive in a world that is unlike the world before. He then puts his knowledge to use to bring science back to the world...hence, roundabout isekai. This series was phenomenal. The allure to continue watching to see what next step toward a world lead by science was addicting. With a promise of S2 coming at the end of the last episode I am looking forward to continuing my addiction with Dr. Stone in the near future.
