for fans
by blue_heart on 2020-05-12 10:15
In this one OVA we get some explanation regarding Yamato's and Kai's past, why they did what they did, and I personally finally understood why Yamato got interested in Mei in the first place. There was also a short Megu-tan and Kai moment which slightly alluded to something possibly growing between them, which was nice. For fans of the anime, and those (like me) who were interested in some more backstory and character development. It was nice, but too bad there's only one OVA episode.
