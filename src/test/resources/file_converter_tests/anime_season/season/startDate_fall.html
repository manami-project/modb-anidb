Sure the plot was simple & easy with sprinkles of interesting events, the artwork especially the backdrops were wonderful here & there. It was rather plain & common in the end, with a few bones to pick though. Parts of the plot felt rushed and unexplained. And the relationship between ML and FL feels weird, undecided romance? teacher-student? ML despite how cool he is, he gives me a bitter taste of dislike in his personality (I dunno maybe he resembles someone).

FL was probably the reason I managed to finish the season. Besides the weird uniform with a cat's eye (mind you I am OK with petanko), found her to be genuine & an interesting character (with a sweet smile) to root for.

All in, its just another of those sword wielding fighter with magic series. For me 4/10 for the effort on the artwork & FL design.
