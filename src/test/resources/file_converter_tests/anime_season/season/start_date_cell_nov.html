* Based on Erich Kästner's work Das doppelte Lottchen (1949).

The story of two twins who were separated at birth and who meet by chance at summer camp, become close friends, and decide to switch places with each other to try to bring their mother and father back together. The Disney film The parent trap was also based on this novel.

reasons

Explanation by Cuan on Sunday, 06.05.2012 12:13

Two stories based 1940s child novels, about two identical (but dissimilar in character) twin sisters who occasionally switch places.

Additionally, both were produced by the same company, and The Two Lottes aired right after St. Clare's finished.
