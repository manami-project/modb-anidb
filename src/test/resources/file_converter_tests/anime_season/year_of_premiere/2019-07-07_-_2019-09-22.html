must see
by soullessgoat on 2019-10-20 01:38
Despite some very serious flaws in the wide angles, this second season basically concludes the build-up from the first season, and does so with exquisite style. Acting, direction, character design, background, ambiance... I've seen a ton of "romcom" anime over the years. This one is, by far, my absolute favorite. Must see. Even if the teasing feels like it gets a little old, or maybe Takagi went just a bit too far for you, do not stop watching.
