* Based on a 4-koma manga, serialised Monthly Newtype since August 2012.

Re-imagines the cast of the Fate/Zero light novel and anime as cute super-deformed characters who open a cafe, led by Saber as the manager.

Note: Screened together with stereoscopic 3D conversion of Fukan Fuukei, the first film from Gekijouban Kara no Kyoukai: The Garden of Sinners, at the ufotable Dining restaurant in Tokyo's Shinjuku ward.
