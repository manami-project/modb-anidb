* Based on the shounen manga by Nishimori Hiroyuki, serialised in Shounen Sunday.

Two boys, Mitsuhashi Takashi and Itou Shinji, meet each other at a salon. It turns out both of them are transferring to a new school and decide to take the opportunity to reinvent themselves. No longer will they be run of the mill high school students, they will become the biggest delinquents Japan has ever seen!

must see
by ymasumac on 2018-05-25 10:19
A well-made, hilarious, feel-good show with extra-long episodes. I'm glad I watched it, and I'll definitely watch it all again. Highly recommended, especially if you're a fan of delinquent comedy :)

They did a DAMN FINE job of conveying seasonal atmosphere. The quality of light captured in the hand-painted scenery is to die for. Several times, I was taken aback by the feel of the air. A virtuoso performance and not at all easy to achieve.

Excellent background music and a wonderful, unique ED which perfectly captures the spirit of this series.

Might have inspired GTO, the Inuyasha character, and a certain fight move from HunterxHunter.
