* Based on a romantic comedy school manga by Mizu Asato.

The teen romantic comedy follows the "indecipherable" daily life of the short and quiet Aharen Reina and Raidou who sits next to her. Aharen is not so good at gauging the distance between people (or personal boundaries), and Raidou initially felt some distance between the two of them. One day, when Raidou picked up the eraser that Aharen had dropped, the distance between them suddenly became uncomfortably close.
